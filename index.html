about unsent

Some words never make it to "send." This is a place for them.

Unsent is an anonymous archive of messages people never delivered — to first loves, lost friends, estranged family, or anyone who still occupies space in someone's chest.

You choose a color that represents the feeling. You write what you couldn't say. It becomes part of something larger — a collective record of unspoken human emotion.

Inspired by The Unsent Project by Rora Blue (2015), which has collected over 5 million messages worldwide.

All submissions are anonymous and cannot be edited or deleted.
